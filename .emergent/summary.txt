<analysis>**original_problem_statement:** 
The user wants to build a comprehensive Accounting, Inventory, and Point of Sale (POS) web application designed for a multi-branch business. The application should be inspired by QuickBooks Online and inFlow Inventory but tailored with unique business logic.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Offline POS:** The POS must function offline and automatically sync data when an internet connection is available, ensuring no data conflicts or double entries.
- **Product Management:** The system needs to handle over 3000 SKUs.
- **Parent/Repack System:** A key feature is the relationship between parent products (sold in bulk, e.g., by the box) and repacks (smaller units from the parent). Selling a repack must automatically and accurately deduct from the parent product's inventory.
- **Multi-Branch Management:** The system must support multiple business locations.
- **Advanced Accounting:** Track daily expenses, cash credits, payables, and receivables. This includes a sophisticated fund management system to track cash across a **Cashier Drawer**, a **Safe** (using a date-based lot system), and a **Bank**.
- **Complex Pricing:** Support multiple pricing tiers (Retail, Wholesale, Special, Government) for different customers.
- **Sales & Purchasing Workflow:** Excel-style interfaces for creating Sales Orders and Purchase Orders with smart product search and on-the-fly product creation.
- **Daily Operations:** A sequential sales log, a daily profit report, and a Close Accounts feature to finalize and lock the day's transactions, generating a detailed printable report.
- **Business Rules:**
    - Currency should be Philippine Peso (₱).
    - A hard rule must prevent selling any item below its capital cost.
    - Receiving a purchase order should automatically update the product's cost price.
    - The system should ask for confirmation to update a pricing scheme if a price is changed manually during a sale.

**User's preferred language**: English
